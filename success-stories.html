Client
Supreme Council for Planning – Oman
Date:

May 10, 2017

Project Brief:

The Royal Directives of His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said stipulate that the future vision “Oman 2040” shall be thoroughly developed and precisely formulated considering wide community consensus and the participation of all social segments to fully realize economic and social realities, objectively foresight the future, and effectively guide planning in the upcoming two decades. LEVENBERT designed a sophisticated process to develop the vision content in a participatory manner starting with status analysis that allowed to build a sense of prioritization of the current key issues at the national level. Next, the focus shifted to the future through a disciplined foresight and scenario making exercise. Those diagnostic and futuristic views allowed for the core phase of determining the national directions and objectives of Oman 2040 vision which took place in a participatory manner were extensive workshops took place in Muscat and all other governorates. Oman 2040 is a national project that aims to create a long-term vision that foresees the future and strive for more progress and development, lined with an ambitious work system that all society segments participate in formulating and executing.

Project Name:
consulting services to support the preparation and formulation of Oman Vision 2040.
Client Profile:

The Council was established in 2012 to develop strategies & policies that enable sustainable development in the Sultanate. Apart from formulating strategies, the council also sets regulations that work in favor of such strategies and drive economic diversity. Under its umbrella, both the Secretariat General and the National Centre for Statistics & Information work closely with the council to deliver results.
